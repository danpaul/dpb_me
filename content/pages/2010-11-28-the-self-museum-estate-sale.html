--- layout: page title: The Self Museum Estate Sale tags: [] status: publish type: page published: true meta: _wp_page_template: default _edit_last: '1' --- The Self Museum Estate Sale is a project of photographs and things.  First, I'm photographing my things (all of them).  I will then advertise all my things and all photographs of those things for sale.  It's sort of an estate sale and an art sale.  It's also a catalog and a collection.  It's a self portrait minus subject, a portrait of the subject via the object.  As a final step, I've decided to compile this into a book which is available for sale on Amazon.com.  I like the idea of being able to sell all my things to somebody.  Also, the idea of compiling everything into a catalog format is appealing to me.  It's really a set of prints, 500 in total, a ream of paper.  The book is available here. Is everything really for sale? Yes, er um, theoretically that is.  If I'm offered the right price, I'd certainly part with anything and, I really do want to minimize my posessions as much as possible so I would many things things for a reasonable or cheap price.  For the sake of the project, I'd sell most things which I actually use on a daily basis for a price which is close to what it would cost me to replace them (second hand).  Many things are theoretically for sale but it would be doubtful that anyone would actually want them (i.e. a half used stick of butter), but are there for the sake of documentation and completeness.
